*Thread Reply:* 1. Go to the pull request tab

  1. Click the one that you want to look at
  2. Read the opening comment on the default 'conversations ' tab to see what should be in the changes
  3. Click on the files tab to see all the proposed changes (red lines are original, green are the new changes/additions)

  5. Once you've given the approval, you go back to the conversations tab, and at the bottom you should see a 'merge' button
